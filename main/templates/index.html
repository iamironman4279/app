Understanding Blood Cancer

Blood cancer, also known as hematologic cancer, encompasses a group of malignancies that affect the blood, bone marrow, and lymphatic system. These cancers originate from abnormal growth and proliferation of blood cells, disrupting the body's normal processes of hematopoiesis and immune function.Among the various types of blood cancer, leukemia, lymphoma, and multiple myeloma are the most prevalent.
